## 💰 PRICING MODEL (2026)

| Plan | Price | Key Features | AI Access |
|------|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Unlimited blocks (solo), 10 guests, 5MB upload, 7-day history | 20 lifetime AI responses TOTAL |
| **Plus** | $10/user/mo (annual) or $12/mo | Unlimited blocks, 100 guests, 30-day history, custom automations | 20 lifetime AI responses TOTAL |
| **Business** | $20/user/mo (annual) or $24/mo | Unlimited AI, Custom Agents, SAML SSO, advanced permissions | FULL AI + Agents included |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Audit log, SCIM provisioning, dedicated CSM, advanced security | FULL AI + Agents + Priority |

### 💡 Key Pricing Insights:
1. **AI is paywalled behind Business ($20/mo)** — this is the BIGGEST change
2. **Separate AI add-on ($10/mo) was killed in May 2025**
3. **Custom Agents** = $10 per 1,000 credits (started May 2026)
4. **Conversion rate**: ~13% free → paid (much higher than SaaS average of 2-5%)
5. **ARPU**: $75-120/year per paid user

2. **AI Monetization**
   - AI bundled into Business/Enterprise (drives upgrade from Plus → Business)

4. **Template Marketplace** (indirect)
   - Community creates templates

   - Not direct revenue but increases stickiness
